Simple Math Lesson

Addition is one of the basic mathematical operations. When we add numbers together, we get a sum.

For example:

  • 2 + 3 = 5
  • 5 + 4 = 9
  • 10 + 15 = 25

Practice Problems

Try these addition problems:

  1. 6 + 7 = ?
  2. 12 + 8 = ?
  3. 25 + 35 = ?

Addition helps us solve many real-world problems, like counting money or measuring distances.
